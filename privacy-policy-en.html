Privacy Policy of LawConnect

Effective date: June 30, 2025

At LawConnect, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when using our services through the website and mobile app. LawConnect complies with Law No. 29733, the Personal Data Protection Law of Peru, its regulatory decree D.S. No. 003-2013-JUS, and other applicable regulations.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Identification details: full name, ID number, address, phone number.
  • Login credentials: username and password.
  • Professional information (for lawyers): profile, specialties, reviews, ratings.
  • App usage history: created cases, contacted lawyers, submitted comments.
  • Technical data: device type, IP address, operating system, and app version.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

Your personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Enable registration and authentication on the platform.
  • Manage and track legal cases.
  • Display professional profiles and search filters.
  • Facilitate communication between client and lawyer.
  • Improve user experience through usage analysis.
  • Comply with applicable legal requirements.

3. Data Sharing

Your data may be shared only under the following conditions:

  • With the lawyer selected by the client, once express consent is given.
  • With competent authorities, in compliance with the law or court order.
  • With third-party service providers helping operate the platform, under strict security and confidentiality standards.

LawConnect does not sell or rent your personal data.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: by registering and using our services, you consent to the processing of your data.
  • Contractual execution: to fulfill obligations arising from the client-lawyer relationship.
  • Legitimate interest: to improve our services, prevent fraud, and ensure platform security.
  • Legal obligations: when we are required by law to retain or process your data.

5. Information Security

We implement technical, administrative, and organizational security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption of confidential information.
  • Secure protocols (HTTPS).
  • Restricted and monitored storage.
  • Ongoing staff training in data protection.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. Afterwards, it will be deleted or anonymized after one (1) year, unless otherwise required by law.

7. User Rights

According to Law No. 29733, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion when no longer needed.
  • Limit or object to data processing under certain circumstances.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at lawconnect@gmail.com

8. Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use technical and analytics cookies to enhance site functionality and user experience. You can manage your preferences in your browser settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be notified through a message on the platform. Continued use implies your acceptance of the updates.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us:
